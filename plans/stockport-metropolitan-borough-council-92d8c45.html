1. Securing a sustainable environment

Local authorities have an extremely important role to play in securing a sustainable environment at local level, and also in contributing to sustainability outcomes at the regional, governmental and international level.

We understand the responsibility we have to lead by example and help the broader community make a positive contribution to the local environment.

We're making positive contributions at local level to help the broader national and international sustainability objectives.
