Sustainability and Climate Action

The Council has been committed to reducing our environmental impact since 2008, when we set a target to reduce our emissions by 40% by 2020 and this target was met. The Council then declared a climate emergency in 2019, committing to achieving net zero emissions by 2030.

Discover what actions the Council is taking towards this goal, and how you can contribute to reducing your environmental impact.
