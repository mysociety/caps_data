Climate Targets

Our Climate Ecological and Emergency Group have identified a series of actions that they would like to take forward in response to declaring a Climate Emergency in Mendip.

Establishing a baseline emissions inventory is the first step in determining how to deliver on the Councils Carbon Emissions Pathway to enable the district to be Net-Zero by 2030. Our targets for each sector are summarised below along with how you can play your part.

Why not sign up to our pledge cards today to help do your bit.

Have you ever worked out your carbon footprint?  Complete the WWF carbon footprint calculator to help you identify what climate actions you can commit to.  

One Home are an organisation who provide practical solutions on all things green. They have identified their "Ten Top Tips for Tacking Climate Change" - a simple guide to enable and encourage low-carbon lifestyle choices.
