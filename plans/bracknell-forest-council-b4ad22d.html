Climate change action plan - potential projects

Greening workforce transport (phase 1)

Moving intra-borough work related journeys from fossil fuel cars to electric vehicles.

Greening borough transport (phase 2)

Creating an electric vehicles car club for residents to access and use greener cars for all travel purposes.

Green Infrastructure Strategy

A Green Infrastructure Strategy is being prepared to help implement the green infrastructure policy of the emerging Bracknell Forest Local Plan (BFLP). The strategy will also help identify opportunities to better link people and wildlife across the landscape in the face of climate change.

Licensing

Manage limitations on taxi emissions by licensing and spot checks.

Heat pumps in parks - a toolkit for cutting carbon and generating income

Installation of heat pumps across parks in the borough.

Recycling of excess heat from cremating process

Recycling of excess heat from cremating process

Decarbonisation funding

Secure decarbonisation funding.

Schools solar photovoltaic panels

Creating an application for general solar photovoltaic panels at all our maintained schools for around £500,000.

London Road landfill site energy hub

There are potential opportunities within this project for funding and green projects.
